# Algorithm Exercise:
## Subject: Riding to Work.

Start.
Do: breathing
Do: checking time
P: At 8:00am if ready, being riding to work.
D: while riding moving be alert of surroundings. If obstacle or potential obstacle adjust speed and course.
D: Whilst riding and coming to a road, prepare to stop.
D: if road has a crossing, stop and press crossing button.
Repeat the above steps as needed whilst on route to work. 
Once near work, locate bike rack.
Stop near bike rack.
Dismount bike.
Chain bike to rack.
Walk in to work.
End.
